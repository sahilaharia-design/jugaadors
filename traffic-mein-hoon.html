How to play

Four steps.
One winner.

Simple to learn. Impossible to put down.

1
Choose your character.
Pick your commuter card. Always late? Always on the wrong side of the platform? Choose wisely.
2
Play event & jugaad cards.
Every round, Mumbai throws something at you. A waterlogged road. A cancelled local. Use your jugaad cards to survive — or make others suffer.
3
Survive the city's chaos.
Block opponents. Play your chaos cards at the right moment. This isn't a race — it's Mumbai. Timing is everything.
4
Reach first. Win.
First one to reach the destination wins. Simple? Everyone else will try to stop you. This is Mumbai, after all.
